INTRO
What I Am All About

My name is Mohi Beyki and I’m a Computer Engineering B.Sc. graduate from University of Tehran, Iran. I work as a software engineer in Cafe Bazaar for more than 4 years now. Cafe Bazaar is the biggest Android Application Store in Iran with over 38 million users.

I'm interested High Performance Computing & Software Engineering. In Cafe Bazaar, I'm currently in infrastructure team and we are responsible for almost every hardware, software & computation need of Cafe Bazaar and Divar and other teams. Divar is a local peer to peer market with more than 10 million active users.

My work is focused on our Object Storage Service and CDNs. I have developed multiple services in infrastructure team, Purge API, Global Server Load Balancer, Distributed Message Queue Platform for Distributed Applications to name a few.
